OpenAI

CEO & Co-founder

Co-founded OpenAI with Elon Musk in 2015 with the mission to ensure artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Under my leadership, OpenAI has developed groundbreaking AI systems including GPT-3, ChatGPT, and DALL-E.

In 2019, we transitioned to a capped-profit model to attract capital while remaining focused on our mission. Our strategic partnership with Microsoft has accelerated our research capabilities and product development.

Y Combinator

President (2014-2019)

Led Y Combinator as President for five years, scaling operations and significantly increasing the accelerator's valuation. During my tenure, I expanded YC's focus into 'hard tech' areas including nuclear energy, supersonic travel, and biotech.

Launched YC Continuity and YC Research to support later-stage companies and fundamental research. Helped nurture companies like Airbnb, Dropbox, and Reddit during their critical growth phases.

Tools for Humanity / Worldcoin

Co-founder

Co-founded Tools for Humanity, the company behind Worldcoin, a project aimed at creating a global identity and financial network. The technology uses iris-scanning to verify "proof of personhood" in an increasingly AI-driven world.

Worldcoin combines a privacy-preserving digital identity solution (World ID) with a digital currency to create a more inclusive and accessible global economy.
